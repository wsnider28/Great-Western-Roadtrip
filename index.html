Fri
Hiouchi → Oregon Coast → Netarts Bay, Tillamook, OR
DriveSightseeingOvernight
▾
9:00 AM🚐Depart Hiouchi north on US-199 to US-101. Cross into Oregon near Brookings.
10:15 AM🌊📍 Samuel H. Boardman Scenic Corridor — plan 3 specific stops: Arch Rock (sea arch framing the ocean), Natural Bridges (short trail to rock arches over churning water — one of the most dramatic spots on the Oregon coast), and Whaleshead Beach (whale-shaped sea stack, beautiful cove). All have pullouts that handle trailers. Budget ~40 min total.
11:00 AM🌅Continue north — pull over at any unsigned scenic lookout between Gold Beach and Bandon. Several gems along this stretch.
12:00 PM⛽Bandon — fuel and stretch.
1:15 PM🍔Lunch near Coos Bay or Florence.
2:00 PM🚐Continue north on US-101 through the Siuslaw coast toward Tillamook.
4:00 PM🏕️📍 Arrive Netarts Bay Garden RV Resort — set up. Bay views, crabbing, morning pastries.
5:00 PM🦀Explore the bay. Try crabbing if the park has rings to loan.
6:30 PM🍽️Drive ~7 miles east into Tillamook for dinner.
1-night stay. Check-in after 2pm. No meeting today. Long coast day — ~280 miles total, budget time generously.

Day 9
Jul 14
Tue
Long Beach → Kalaloch / Tree of Life → Ruby Beach → Hard Rain Café, Hoh
Drive⚠️ MeetingSightseeingOvernight
▾
9:00 AM🚐Depart Long Beach north on US-101. ~90 miles to Kalaloch, ~1.75 hrs convoy.
10:45 AM🌳📍 Kalaloch — Tree of Life — park in day-use area (free, no park pass needed). Short trail down to the beach, walk a couple minutes north. A Sitka spruce somehow suspended over an eroded cave — defying gravity since forever. ⚠️ Admire from a distance, don't climb. Quick stop ~30 min.
11:20 AM🌊📍 Ruby Beach (~12 min north). Large pullout lot — trailers fine. Dramatic sea stacks, massive ancient driftwood logs, wild Washington coast. Plan 45 min–1 hr here.
12:30 PM🍔Picnic at Ruby Beach or continue to Forks for lunch.
1:15 PM⛽📍 Forks — fuel up, lunch if not done at Ruby Beach. Last real services before Hard Rain Café.
2:00 PM🌿Turn south on Upper Hoh Rd (~19 miles to Hard Rain).
2:30 PM🏕️📍 Arrive Hard Rain Café RV Park — set up. Check-in after 1pm.
3:00 PM🌲Short walk on the Hoh River Trail from camp — taste the rainforest air.
3:30 PM📡Back at camp. Starlink up.
4:00 PM💻🔴 Tuesday meeting (4:00–5:45 PM Pacific) — at camp.
6:00 PM🍔Dinner at the Hard Rain Café — the Bigfoot Burger is legendary.
2-night stay. Water & 30-amp electric only — NO sewer. Tanks should be dumped from Long Beach.
